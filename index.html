Introduction

I am a Data Engineer and Cloud Engineer with solid experience in AWS, Infrastructure as Code (Terraform), Python, CI/CD, observability, and automation. I specialize in building scalable and cost-efficient cloud architectures, data lakes, and big data pipelines, helping organizations improve performance and streamline operations.

At NTT DATA (Banco Itaú), I contributed to the democratization of data by developing and automating pipelines, building and optimizing data lakes with millions of records using Spark, Glue, and EMR, and integrating AWS services such as DynamoDB and Step Functions. I also implemented monitoring solutions with Datadog, Prometheus, and Grafana to ensure high availability and reliability.

Previously, at PowerOfData, I optimized a data lake with more than 1 billion records, improving performance by 40% and automating cloud deployments with CI/CD on AWS. My focus has always been on performance, cost optimization, and enabling strategic insights from large-scale data.

I am committed to continuous learning, currently pursuing postgraduate studies in Cloud Computing and preparing for AWS certifications (Solutions Architect Associate and Data Analytics Specialty). With adaptability, problem-solving mindset, and clear communication, I aim to deliver efficient cloud solutions that support both engineering teams and business growth.
